01 — About

hi!

Boston, Mass.
⌁ Analyst · Student

I'm Omar — a Malaysian Master's student in Applied Business Analytics at Boston University, graduating in May 2026. Before this, I studied Operations and Business Analytics at the University of Oregon's Lundquist College of Business.

I care about the quiet part of analytics; the scrubbing, the reshaping, the part where messy data starts to tell a coherent story. Whether it's palm oil yields in Malaysia, Porsche's customer relationship data, or the evolution of AI/ML requirements in job postings, the fun is in finding a clean signal inside all the noise.

I've spent time doing that at SD Guthrie, Porsche Malaysia, and the University of Oregon's Office of Finance and Administration, where I helped model enterprise architecture for the Banner system using ArchiMate.

Outside of spreadsheets and regressions, I'm into building things, this site, forecasting models, whatever keeps my hands and brain going. I like work that sits at the intersection of clean data and good design: dashboards that are actually readable, reports people actually open, websites with some umph.

Right now I'm working through a BU course project on the Lightcast job postings dataset — analyzing how AI/ML skills are showing up across industries and what that means for people entering business analytics today. Linked on the Projects page.

If any of that lines up with what your team is building, let's talk.
